Business Performance Dashboard
Overview

The Business Performance Dashboard provides a comprehensive and interactive visualization of key sales performance metrics. Designed to facilitate quick insights and data-driven decision-making, this dashboard is an essential tool for understanding the overall health and trends in business sales.

Key Features:
  • Interactive Filters: Allows users to filter data by specific countries, business segments, products, and discount bands.
  • Sales Performance by Month: Line chart comparing monthly sales for the years 2014 and 2013.
  • Key Sales Metrics: Displays total sales, gross sales, gross amount, and profit values.
  • Product and Segment Analysis: Bar chart of product count by segment.
  • Country Sales Comparison: Horizontal bar chart comparing sales for 2013 and 2014 across different countries.

This Business Performance Dashboard is an invaluable tool for sales managers, analysts, and business executives. By providing a detailed and interactive view of sales data, it empowers users to make informed decisions, identify growth opportunities, and optimize business strategies.

Sales Dashboard
Overview

The Sales Dashboard offers a detailed and interactive visualization of key sales metrics, providing valuable insights into sales performance and trends. It is designed to help sales managers and analysts track sales activities, identify opportunities, and make data-driven decisions.

Key Features:
  • Interactive Filters: Users can filter data by year, city, product name, and channel to view specific subsets of the sales data.
  • Sales and Profit Metrics: Displays key sales metrics such as total sales, profit, and products sold.
  • Sales by City: A pie chart showing the distribution of sales across different cities.
  • Sales by Product Name: A bar chart comparing sales and sales from the previous year (PY) by product name.
  • Sales by Customer: A horizontal bar chart comparing sales and sales from the previous year (PY) by customer.
  • Sales by Month: A bar chart showing monthly sales compared to the previous year's sales.
  • Profit by Channel: A line chart comparing profit and profit from the previous year (LY) by sales channel.

This Sales Dashboard is an essential tool for sales teams and executives, providing a comprehensive view of sales performance and enabling informed decision-making to drive business growth and success.

Call Analysis Dashboard
Overview

The Call Analysis Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of call center operations, highlighting key metrics and visualizations that offer insights into call volumes, durations, satisfaction scores, and more. This dashboard is essential for monitoring and improving call center performance.

Key Features:
  • Total Calls: Displays the total number of calls handled.
  • Average Call Duration: Shows the average duration of calls.
  • Total Call Duration: Indicates the total duration of all calls in minutes.
  • Average Satisfaction Score: Displays the average satisfaction score of the calls.
  • Total Calls by Reason: A tree map showing the distribution of calls by reason (e.g., billing questions, payments, service outages).
  • Total Calls by Channel: A pie chart displaying the distribution of calls by channel (e.g., call center, chatbot, email, web).
  • Total Calls by Sentiment: A bar chart showing the distribution of calls by sentiment (e.g., positive, negative, neutral).
  • Total Calls by Call Center City: A bar chart displaying the distribution of calls by the city where the call center is located.
  • Geographic Distribution: A map showing the geographic distribution of call volumes.

This Call Analysis Dashboard is a powerful tool for call center managers and analysts, providing actionable insights to help improve customer service, identify trends, and optimize call center performance.
